Mundus Stone Values

Accurate as of version 2.2.10

The Thief grants 11% weapon and spell crit chance at all levels
The Shadow grants 12% weapon and spell crit damage at all levels

The Math

The Lady / Lover: Bonus Resistance = 30 * Level
The Mage / Tower: Bonus Attribute = 20 * Level
The Lord: Bonus Health = 20 * Level * 1.1 (equivalently, 22 * Level)
The Apprentice / Warrior: Bonus Damage = 2.54 * Level

Veteran ranks are simply treated as additive levels on top of 50 (e.g. VR13 is like "level 63")
